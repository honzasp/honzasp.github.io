Krt is one of the largest JavaScript-based games I have made. Players engage in a battle of circular tanks, shooting both the opponents to receive points and the blocky surroundings to supply them with energy and mass vital for survival.

The game makes use of many cutting-edge Web APIs, notably Audio API and Web Workers, to get as much as possible from modern browsers.

Of possible interest is also the game physics, detecting and solving the collisions of the grid and the objects, and the noise algorithm used to generate unlimited amount of random maps. This algorithm can be executed in a background thread, speeding up the game initialization.
